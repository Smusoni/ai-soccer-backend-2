# SOTA: One-Page Investor Summary

## The Opportunity

**Market**: Soccer talent evaluation ($500M TAM, 1,200+ pro clubs, 10,000+ scouts)
**Problem**: Talent evaluation takes 30+ hours per candidate, costs $5K-15K/month
**Solution**: AI video analysis in 90 seconds with quantified metrics

## Product

**What**: Upload game or training video → Get AI analysis + highlights in 90 seconds
- Game mode: Pass completion %, first touch grade, game awareness, defensive actions
- Training mode: Technical execution, movement quality, consistency, recommended drills
- Both modes: AI-extracted highlights with timestamps

**Tech Stack**: Node.js + Express + GPT-4o vision + Cloudinary
**Cost per analysis**: $0.10 (AI processing + infrastructure)
**Sell for**: $5-15 per analysis OR $99-299/month subscription
