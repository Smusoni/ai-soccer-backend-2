- Missing **quantified metrics**: pass completion %, first touch grades, decision-making scores

This happens **1,200+ times per year** at each of the 1,200+ professional clubs globally. **$500M+ market** making decisions blind.

### Our Solution

**SOTA** = AI-Powered Talent Evaluation in 90 Seconds

**What we built:**
1. **Upload game OR training video**
2. **AI analyzes 10 frames across the duration**
3. **Get structured metrics + AI-extracted highlights**

**Game Analysis Output:**
- Pass completion %, first touch grade (1-10)
- Game awareness (positioning, scanning, decision quality)
- Defensive actions (tackles, interceptions, success rate)
- Critical moments with timestamps + assessments
- Alternative decisions (what else could they have done)
- Overall grade (1-10)

**Training Analysis Output:**
- Session focus detection
- Technical execution grade
- Movement quality + athleticism scores
- Decision-making under pressure
- Consistency assessment
- Recommended drills by weakness
- Highlights with timestamps

**Cost per analysis**: $0.10
**Sell for**: $5-15 per analysis OR $99-299/month
